## How Republicans Stole Two Supreme Court Seats

In March 2016, President Obama nominated Merrick Garland to the Supreme Court with 293 days remaining in his term. Senate Republicans refused to hold hearings, claiming voters should decide in the upcoming election.

In September 2020, Justice Ruth Bader Ginsburg died with just 46 days before the election. Senate Republicans rushed through Amy Coney Barrett's confirmation in record time—just 30 days from nomination to confirmation.

<!--more-->

The hypocrisy was breathtaking. The theft was successful. And the Supreme Court's legitimacy has never recovered.

The confirmation process is broken beyond repair—and Court reform is the only remedy.
