## üí° Pro Tips for Maximum Impact

### Writing Instagram Captions

**Formula:**
1. **Hook** (first line) - Shocking fact or question
2. **Context** (2-3 sentences) - Why it matters
3. **Proof** (2-3 sentences) - Evidence/details
4. **Call to action** - "Link in bio," "Share if you agree," etc.
5. **Hashtags** - 9-12 relevant tags

**Example Hook Lines:**
- "Justice Thomas accepted $4.75 MILLION in gifts."
- "Six justices appointed by presidents who LOST the popular vote."
- "The Supreme Court just granted presidents immunity for crimes."
- "2013: Voting Rights Act gutted. 2025: 94 restrictive voting laws passed."

### Best Times to Post

Research shows best engagement on Instagram:
- **Weekdays**: 11am - 2pm EST (lunch break scrolling)
- **Evenings**: 7pm - 9pm EST (after work/dinner)
- **Best days**: Tuesday, Wednesday, Thursday
- **Avoid**: Early morning, very late night

Start by posting around **12pm EST** on weekdays.
